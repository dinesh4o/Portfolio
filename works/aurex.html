Challenges.

Challenges.

Arjuna is a skilled design engineer with a strong portfolio, but his online presence didn’t reflect the quality of his work. He needed a personal site that could showcase his experience, process, and personality — while also making it easy for potential clients or teams to reach out. The old layout felt flat, lacked structure, and didn’t support storytelling.

Solutions.

Solutions.

We built a sleek one-page website in Framer, designed to highlight work without feeling overwhelming. The layout is built around clarity — short project summaries, smooth scroll sections, and a contact CTA always within reach. We also added a CMS to manage future case studies and blog posts, making it easy to scale over time. The tone and visuals were inspired by the name "Arjuna" — calm, focused, and intentional.

Results.

Results.

Arjuna’s site now reflects his skill and focus — helping him stand out in a competitive industry and connect with more aligned opportunities.
